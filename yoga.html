Yoga Routines

9 beginner‑friendly poses with photos and quick cues.

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Stand tall with feet hip‑width. Improves posture and grounding.

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Lengthens the spine and stretches hamstrings, calves and shoulders.

Child’s Pose (Balasana)

Restorative posture to relax back and hips while calming the mind.

Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I)

Strengthens legs, improves focus, and opens the chest.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Strengthens back and opens chest to improve posture.

Cat‑Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana/Bitilasana)

Flow between arching and rounding the back to warm up the spine.

Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)

Opens chest and strengthens glutes, hamstrings, and lower back.

Tree Pose (Vrksasana)

Improves balance and focus while strengthening ankles and calves.

Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)

Stretches hamstrings, spine, and calms the nervous system.
