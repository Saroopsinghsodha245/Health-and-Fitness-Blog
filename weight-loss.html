Weight Loss Tips

10 smart, sustainable strategies to make progress and keep it.

Load Up on Fiber

Veggies, fruit, legumes & whole grains keep you full longer with fewer calories.

Walk 30 Minutes

Daily steps add up—great for fat loss, mood, and recovery.

Track What You Eat

Use a simple log or app. Awareness helps you spot easy wins.

Drink Water First

A glass before meals can reduce overeating and support hydration.

Eat Slowly & Mindfully

Put the fork down between bites; stop at satisfied, not stuffed.

Prioritize Sleep

7–9 hours supports appetite hormones, energy, and fat loss.

Cut Sugary Drinks

Swap soda/juice for water or tea; keep ultra‑processed snacks occasional.

Plan Ahead

Prep basics once. Planned meals beat last‑minute takeout.

Lift to Keep Muscle

2–3 weekly sessions protect muscle and metabolism while losing fat.

Set Realistic Goals

Aim for 0.25–0.75 kg per week. Celebrate consistency and small wins.
