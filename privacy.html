This policy explains our practices for the Health & Fitness blog.

Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how the Health & Fitness blog (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or interact with our content.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide (e.g., name, email, phone) when you use our contact form.
  • Content in messages or feedback you send us.
  • Usage data such as pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser info (see Cookies).

How We Use Information

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website and content.
  • Prevent misuse and keep the site secure.

Legal Basis (EU/UK visitors)

If you are in the EU/UK, we rely on these legal bases:

  • Consent (e.g., optional cookies, newsletter sign‑ups).
  • Legitimate interests (site security, basic analytics, improving content).
  • Contract/Pre‑contract (when you request information or services).

Cookies & Analytics

We may use essential cookies to make the site work and basic analytics to understand usage and improve content. You can usually manage cookies in your browser settings. If we add any non‑essential cookies in the future, we’ll request consent where required.

Email & Communications

If you email us or submit a form, we’ll use your details to reply. We don’t sell your information. You may ask us to stop contacting you at any time.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law. When data is no longer needed, we aim to delete or anonymize it responsibly.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information. No online service can be 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

Children’s Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data, and to object or withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

Third‑Party Links

Our website may link to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version. Significant changes will be highlighted on this page.

Questions about this policy? Email us at contact@yourdomain.com.
