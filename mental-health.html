Mental Wellness

Simple daily habits to reduce stress and support your mind.

Meditate 10 Minutes

Sit quietly and focus on your breath. Short daily sessions reduce stress and improve focus.

Take Tech‑Free Breaks

Schedule short breaks away from screens to rest your eyes and calm your mind.

Spend Time in Nature

A 20–30 minute walk outdoors can lower stress and boost mood.

Reach Out & Talk

Share how you feel with a trusted friend or a professional. Connection helps.

Gratitude Journal

Write 3 good things each day. It trains your brain to notice the positives.

Calming Music

Play gentle music or nature sounds to unwind and improve sleep quality.
