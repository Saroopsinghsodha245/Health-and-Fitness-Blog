Nutrition Guide

Photo tips you can follow today—simple, tasty, sustainable.

Hydrate Well

Aim for ~8 glasses/day. Keep a bottle nearby and sip regularly.

Protein at Each Meal

Eggs, beans, fish, chicken or tofu help keep you fuller and support recovery.

Prioritize Fiber

Vegetables, fruits, legumes and whole grains support gut health and fullness.

Choose Whole Grains

Oats, brown rice and whole‑grain breads give longer‑lasting energy.

Include Healthy Fats

Avocado, nuts, seeds and olive oil support hormones and satisfaction.

Mind Portions, Eat Slowly

Pause between bites and stop at satisfied, not stuffed.

Cut Added Sugar

Swap sugary drinks for water or tea; enjoy sweets in small portions.

Read Food Labels

Check sugar, sodium and fiber. Shorter ingredient lists are often better.

Prep Simple Staples

Cook grains, roast veggies, boil eggs once—build fast meals all week.

Eat the Rainbow

Different colors = different nutrients. Aim for 3–5 colors per plate.

Get Omega‑3s Weekly

Fatty fish or plant sources (flax, chia, walnuts) support heart & brain.

Calcium + Vitamin D

Dairy or fortified alternatives help bones; add safe sunlight as advised.

Cook Smart

Grill, bake, steam or air‑fry more often than deep‑fry.

Choose Whole Fruit

Whole fruit adds fiber and keeps you fuller compared to juice.
